PLAN MY NIGHT

VISIT SITE (allow load time)

Are you an indecisive planner? Or have friends that are always just up for "whatever"? Then this app is for you! Plan My Night randomly generates plans for you, including two locations and a local event. This also features a meetup tool, which will find the perfect venue located conveniently between you and a friend.

Built on Ruby on Rails

FEATURES:

  • Utilizes multiple APIs to generate your perfect night! APIs include Weather API, Google Maps, Eventbrite, Geocoder, Stripe, and Yelp.
  • Designed with very user-friendly interface and visually appealing graphics
  • Site includes complex algorithms to generate a list of plans that are within walking distance (1 mi) of one another
  • Site allows users to save plans easily to quickly share and keep track of plans
